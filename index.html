Exam Techniques

  1. Spaced Repetition Intervals: Review flashcards 1 day, 3 days, and 7 days after learning.
  2. Active Recall: Brain-dump concepts onto a blank sheet before looking at notes.
  3. Pomodoro Cycles: Work in targeted 25-minute milestones with 5-minute offline breaks.
